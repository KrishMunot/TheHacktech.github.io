What even?

Hacktech is a 36 hour event where students collaborate to create "hacks": projects that bring technologies together in an innovative way.

Who even?

500 hackers, 100-200 sponsors, organizers, and judges

When even?

Feb 26-28, 2016

Where even?

California Market Center: 110 E 9th St A727, Los Angeles, CA 90079

How even?

All you have to do is fill out the application form when it is released! For now, we have a pre-registration form, where you can sing up to be notified when applications open.

FAQ

Who can come?

Hacktech is open to any student enrolled in high school or college over 18, anywhere in the world.

How much will it cost?

Nothing! Hacktech is absolutely free for all accepted hackers. We'll be giving you all the food, caffeine, swag, space, and WiFi you need to make your ideas a reality. We will also be offering travel reimbursements for those who qualify.

What should I bring?

A student ID, a laptop, and a charger are the only necessities. We recommend you bring a change of clothes, toiletries, and a sleeping bag as well!

How will I get there?

We will be providing buses to certain universities, but if there isn't one for your school, we will reimburse you for your transportation costs.

How large can a team be? What if I don't have one?

Teams can have up to 5 members. Don't worry if you don't already have one; there will be plenty of other people who don't have teams either, and we'll help you find a team!

What if I've never been to a hackathon before?

Don't worry, they're freaking awesome. Hacktech is meant to be a learning experience. We want you to come here, have fun, and leave having learned something! We'll have workshops and activities and all sorts of other events, so don't be scared!

What can I make? What if I don't have an idea?

Absolutely anything you want. You can make a website, or a mobile application, or even tinker with some hardware! It's all up to you. Don't worry if you don't have an idea coming in; plenty of good ideas will be thought up on-site, and maybe you'll meet some new teammates that have ideas!

What about hardware?

We'll have hardware available for you to work with, like Oculus. Feel free to bring your own, if you own something you would like to code for.

When will I hear back?

We'll be getting back to you in late-January/early-February, so hang tight until then!

What if I'm a Caltech student?

Fill out the application now as well! Everyone who applies can participate, but we may not be able to offer you space at the venue.
